### liquidateCreditAccount

```solidity

    address creditAccount,
    CollateralDebtData calldata collateralDebtData,
    address to,
    bool isExpired
) external returns (uint256 remainingFunds, uint256 loss)
```

Liquidates a credit account.

<ParamField path="creditAccount" type="address" required>
  The credit account to liquidate
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="collateralDebtData" type="CollateralDebtData" required>
  Collateral and debt data for the account
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="to" type="address" required>
  Address to send remaining funds to
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="isExpired" type="bool" required>
  Whether the account is being liquidated due to expiration
</ParamField>

<ResponseField name="remainingFunds" type="uint256">
  Amount of funds remaining after liquidation
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="loss" type="uint256">
  Amount of loss incurred during liquidation
</ResponseField>
